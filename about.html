My Story

My passion for art inspires my work and, through digital products, I aim to evoke emotions and beauty. I have always been interested in users behaviours, storytelling and brand identity; in fact, the user-centric philosophy is what led me to design.

My background in fashion has taught me to work in fast-paced environments, where I have forecasted the users' needs based on past trends in turnover, internal movement and company's growth.

By having insights into human decision-making behaviours, I was able to find solutions that work around clients.
